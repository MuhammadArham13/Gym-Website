About Us

Our History

The Best CrossFit Gym was founded by a small group of coaches who believed New York City deserved a box built on real coaching, honest programming, and a community that pushes each other to be better every single day. What started as a single room with a few barbells has grown into one of the most respected training facilities in the city.

Today, we bring together athletes of every level — from first-time gym-goers to competitive lifters — under one roof. Our mission has never changed: build people up, inside the gym and out, through hard work, accountability, and genuine support.
